Nutrition for a Healthy Life

Good nutrition helps you stay energetic, strong and disease-free.

What is Nutrition?

Nutrition is the process of taking in food and using it for growth, energy and maintaining good health. A balanced diet provides the body with essential nutrients needed for daily functioning.

Types of Nutrients

Carbohydrates

Provide energy for daily activities.

Proteins

Help in body building and muscle repair.

Fats

Support brain function and store energy.

Vitamins

Boost immunity and support body functions.

Minerals

Maintain strong bones and blood health.

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, grains, protein, dairy and healthy fats. It provides the body with all necessary nutrients.

Benefits of Good Nutrition

  • Boosts immunity
  • Improves brain function
  • Maintains body weight
  • Provides energy
  • Supports mental health

Healthy Eating Tips

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Eat fruits daily
  • Avoid junk food
  • Reduce sugar intake
  • Eat on time
